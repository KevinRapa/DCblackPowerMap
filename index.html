THE D.C. BLACK POWER CHRONICLES

This project is the culmination of many research hours performed by staff and students of the University of Maryland, Baltimore County. Use this tool to explore the history of African American advancement originating in Washington D.C.

  • Navigate through events using the sliders, buttons, and icons on the map.
  • View the images and descriptions in the other pane.
  • Click the 'To street view' button to see a modern day view of the event site.
  • You may get a better view of the image by clicking it.
  • Toggle filters by event type using the legend icons.

Thanks for visiting!
